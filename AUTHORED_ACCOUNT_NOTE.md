# Account Authorship Wording

Use this wording in the generated Community Impact Record:

Prepared Using: Bellbrook Advocacy Portal

Account Author: [Resident Name]

Account Status: First-person resident account

Formatting Assistance: Bellbrook Advocacy Portal

Ownership: The contents remain the account and observations of the resident.

Use this wording in cover letters:

This attached record is my account. The Bellbrook Advocacy Portal assisted in formatting and preserving the information. The contents remain my account and observations.
