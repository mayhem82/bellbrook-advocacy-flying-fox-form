# Recipient Questions Library

## Purpose

Reusable recipient-specific question sets generated from resident evidence and Bellbrook contextual material.

---

## Council Questions

- Will a Bellbrook Flying-fox Camp Management Plan be developed?
- If not, why not?
- What outputs resulted from the August–September 2025 consultation process?
- What responses were received from state agencies?
- What funding applications have been submitted for Bellbrook?

---

## Mayor / Councillor Questions

- Will you support development of a Bellbrook management plan?
- Will you request a formal briefing on Bellbrook?
- Will you place the matter before council again?
- What specific action will you personally take?

---

## State MP Questions

- Has NSW DCCEEW responded to Kempsey Shire Council?
- What options exist when a council refuses to develop a management plan?
- Does government consider Bellbrook an active or historic camp?
- What assistance can be provided to affected residents?

---

## Federal MP Questions

- What federal mechanisms may exist for communities affected by listed species?
- What role does EPBC listing create in this situation?
- Has the federal environment portfolio considered Bellbrook?
- Can federal support pathways be identified?

---

## NSW Health Questions

- Has any health assessment been undertaken?
- Does NSW Health hold records relating to Bellbrook?
- What response was provided to council correspondence?
- Are sleep disruption and wellbeing impacts being monitored?

---

## Environment Minister / DCCEEW Questions

- Can Bellbrook access grant funding without a management plan?
- What options exist if council refuses to develop a plan?
- Is Bellbrook correctly classified within departmental records?
- Has the department provided advice regarding Bellbrook?

---

## NSW Ombudsman Questions

- Were available administrative pathways properly considered?
- Why was the management plan request declined?
- Why did the matter not return to the October 2025 agenda?
- What became of consultation outputs?
- Were residents adequately informed of outcomes?

---

## Media Questions

- Why has six and a half years of process produced no documented outcome?
- Why can Bellbrook not access the available funding pathway?
- Why is Bellbrook classified as historic while residents report ongoing impacts?
- What evidence exists and what does it show?

---

## Usage Rule

Questions should be presented as portal-generated inquiry prompts.

They are not statements of fact by the resident unless the resident explicitly adopts them.
