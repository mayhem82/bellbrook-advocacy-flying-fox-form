# Transparency Statement Template

## Purpose

Ensure residents can clearly distinguish between their own testimony and material generated by the Bellbrook Advocacy Portal.

## Resident Authored Content

The following sections originate from the resident:

- Community Impact Record
- Personal testimony
- Personal observations
- Personal experiences
- Personal requested actions
- Contact history described by the resident

## Portal Generated Content

The following sections are generated by the Bellbrook Advocacy Portal:

- Cover letter templates
- Recipient guidance attachments
- Recipient-specific briefing notes
- Recipient-specific questions
- Submission instructions
- Response tracking guidance

## Attribution Rule

Portal-generated content must not be represented as personal testimony unless the resident explicitly adopts, edits, or incorporates that material.

## Resident Control

Residents may:

- Edit generated content
- Remove generated content
- Replace generated content
- Submit only their own testimony if they choose

## Package Ownership

The resident remains the owner of their testimony.

The portal provides formatting, translation, guidance, and submission support.

## Disclosure Statement

This package contains both resident-authored material and portal-generated support material.

The purpose of the generated material is to assist communication with the selected recipient.

It should not be interpreted as legal advice, professional representation, or a statement made by the resident unless the resident expressly adopts that content.
