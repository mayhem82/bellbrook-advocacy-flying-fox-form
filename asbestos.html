ASBESTOS COMMUNITY RECORD

For resident accounts about suspected or reported asbestos concern at or near 15-17 Main Street, Bellbrook.

Purpose

This module records what residents saw, heard, photographed, reported, or were told. It does not confirm contamination. It does not say legal action has commenced. It creates an account that can be sent, archived, compared, and followed up.

Safety note

Do not disturb suspected asbestos material.
Do not touch, sweep, break, collect, bag, move, or handle it. Record what you saw and report it through the proper authority.

What residents may ask for

  • Inspection and written result
  • Testing or confirmation where required
  • Visible safety controls if material is accessible
  • Resident notification where needed
  • Written response and reference number
  • Record of what authority was contacted and when
