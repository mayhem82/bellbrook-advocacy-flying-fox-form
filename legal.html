What these portals are

NSW Community Resilience Portals are community-run tools built to help residents put their own experiences into a clear, consistent format. Each community - such as Bellbrook or Taylors Arm - has its own portal, scoped to its own shire. None of them are a government service, a law firm, or a substitute for professional legal, health, or financial advice. None of them represent residents in any formal, legal, or official capacity.

This is not legal advice

Nothing on this portal - including the facts pages, cover letters, recipient guidance, or requested actions - is legal advice. If you are considering a legal question, a dispute, or a matter that could affect your legal rights, seek advice from a qualified solicitor.

Your information and privacy

Every community portal is built to keep as much control as possible in the hands of the resident:

  • The form runs in your own browser. Nothing you type is sent anywhere automatically.
  • Some of the details a resident may choose to describe - such as stress, sleep disruption, or health concerns - are sensitive personal information. You decide whether to include them, and nothing is required beyond what you choose to provide.
  • Your record is only sent to that community's archive if you actively choose to email an archive receipt. That email goes to the archive address shown on the relevant form.
  • No portal sells, shares, or uses your information for any purpose beyond the community record process described on the How Community Records Work page, and only to the extent the archive permission you selected allows.
  • If you want an archived copy corrected, updated, or removed, contact the archive using the email address shown on the form you used, referencing the date and details of your submission.

Accuracy, fairness, and correction

Resident accounts on this portal are personal testimony - what someone saw, experienced, or was told. The portal does not independently investigate or verify the factual claims residents make, and publishing or sending a record through this portal does not mean any claim in it has been confirmed, endorsed, or found to be true by any authority.

Facts pages on this portal (for example, background information provided before a resident starts a record) are prepared in good faith from information available to residents and public correspondence, and are presented as the community's understanding of events rather than a finding of fact. If you are a person or organisation named on this portal and believe something written about you is inaccurate or unfair, contact the relevant archive email address shown on the page concerned so it can be reviewed and corrected if needed.

No liability

This portal is provided as a free community tool, without warranty of any kind. To the extent permitted by law, nobody involved in building or operating it accepts liability for how residents, recipients, or any other person uses the records, letters, or other material it generates.

Contact

Questions about privacy, accuracy, or anything else on this page can be sent to the relevant archive email address shown on the Bellbrook Flying Fox Camp, Taylors Arm Flying Fox, or Community Discovery pages.
