Resident submission output rule:

The Bellbrook Flying Fox Community Impact Record must read as a first-person resident submission.

Use first person for generated framing:
- I report
- I have experienced
- I have contacted
- I am asking for
- I give permission

Do not use third person in the resident submission:
- The resident reports
- The resident requests
- The resident has experienced

Resident-typed account fields must remain verbatim.

Do not rewrite resident-written text.
Do not summarise resident-written text.
Do not clean up tone, grammar, spelling, wording, or structure.
Do not convert resident-written text into third person.
Do not paraphrase lived experience.

The portal may only:
- place the resident's exact words under headings
- add labels around the account
- list selected checkbox impacts
- add a short portal note at the bottom
- generate a cover letter separately

Portal metadata belongs at the bottom as a short note.

Empty fields should not dominate the submission.
