UNDER CONSTRUCTION

This portal is being built for Turners Flat, a locality along Armidale Road in the Macleay Valley, Kempsey Shire. It is not ready for public use and does not yet identify a confirmed scope, location, person, or finding.

BUILD-STAGE PORTAL

This portal and its one section are visible so they can be built, tested, reviewed, and corrected. Nothing here should be treated as a finished or public-facing community resource yet.

About this portal

The Turners Flat Community Resilience Portal is being built to help residents of Turners Flat turn local experiences, concerns, and observations into clear records that can be saved, shared, and followed up. It is its own community portal, separate from the Bellbrook Community Resilience Portal and other NSW Community Resilience Portals, even though Turners Flat sits within the same Kempsey Shire local government area as Bellbrook.

How this works

This portal shares the same underlying framework as other NSW Community Resilience Portals. If you would like to understand how the framework reviews community records, groups similar issues, and separates different types of evidence, read How Community Records Work.
